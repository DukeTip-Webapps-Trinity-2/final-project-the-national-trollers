Flathead Lake National Park

Flathead Lake National Park is located in Northwest Montana. It is a bit south of Glacier National Park and is the 60th national park in the U.S. It was founded in 2018 and is brand new. The park contains first-class experiences for summer and winter where you can ski, snowboard, hike, camp, fish, and other things. The park contained over 100,000 visitors on opening day.

About the Park

Flathead Lake National Park, where you can climb mountains, swim lengths, hike trails, learn about nature, and pretty much anything to do with nature. As the nations newest park, we take pride in how we do things. So come on. Journey into the untamed wilderness and stake your claim in the brand new history of this park. Because as we said earlier, this place is our home, and we want it to be yours too.

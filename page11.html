Hiking

There are so many different trails at FLNP, whether it be the easy Cub Walk or the challenging Canyon Glacier Trail. The crowd favorite is the medium Glacier Loop, a 5.5 mile trek where you see majestic mountains and the world famous Valley of the 11 Peaks.

Mountaineering

There are various options for climbing here in the Rockies. The park's highest peak is Emerald Peak, sitting at 30,798 feet. It is widely considered the hardest climb in the lower 48 and only 23 people have done it before. There are 12 peaks at over 12,000 ft in the park.

Camping

New camping sites in the park are all over the place. Make sure to throw your trash away. Each campsite should be near a creek and comes with its own bonfire.

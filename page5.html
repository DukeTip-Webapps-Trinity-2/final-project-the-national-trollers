Glacier Park International Airport-GPI (Kalispell, MT)

Glacier Park International is the closest airport to the park, but it is the smallest on the list. There are 4 airlines at Glacier Park, Delta United, Alaska, and Allegiant. Nonstop destinations include Atlanta, Chicago, Minneapolis, Denver, Salt Lake City, Seattle, Portland, and others...

Missoula International Airport-MSO (Missoula, MT)

Missoula International is about 2 hours away from the park. It has 5 airlines, Delta, United, American, Allegiant, and Alaska. Nonstop service includes Oakland, Atlanta, Chicago, Minneapolis, Dallas, Phoenix, and others...

Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport-BZN (Bozeman, MT)

Bozeman Yellowstone International is the largest airport in Montana. It is home to six airlines, including Delta, United, American, JetBlue, Alaska, and Frontier. Nonstop service includes Las Vegas, New York, Houston, Atlanta, Seattle, Portland, and others...

Spokane International Airport-GEG (Spokane, WA)

Spokane International is about 4 and a half hours away, but it is a little bit bigger than Bozeman, which is a little shorter of a drive. Airlines include Alaska, American, Delta, United, Frontier, and Southwest. Nonstop service includes Boise, Chicago Midway, San Diego, Sacramento, San Jose, and others...

Calgary International Airport-YYC (Calgary, Canada)

Calgary International is one of the largest airports in North America and serves much more destinations than the other four, but it is in Canada and it is a 5 1/2 hr drive. This would be a great option if you are visiting other parks in Canada like Banff National Park, Waterton Lakes National Park, and others... The flight is probably cheaper into Calgary too...
